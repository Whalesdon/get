Discounts and revenue share per affiliate link

Holders of 500,000+ $DUPE are able to get a wide range of benefits and discounts. Discounts include a 50% deduction on token launch fees. Holders can also generate a unique referral link which gives the receiver the same 50% discount for deploying, and holders can claim a 70% revenue share for tokens deployed using their unique referral.

Example: Joe shares his referral link to Harry. Harry launches a coin using the 0.2 eth plan. Because of the discount Harry received from Joe's referral, Harry only pays 0.1 eth for the plan. Because Harry used Joe's referral, Joe is able to claim 70% (0.14 eth) from the deployment fee.

Revenue share from $DUPE coin taxes

Holders of 200,000+ $DUPE are able to claim 10% of tax fees $DUPE coin collects on a weekly basis which will be distributed proportionally to the amount of tokens they hold.Example: Joe holds 200,000 $DUPE, Harry holds 250,000 $DUPE. $DUPE generates 100 eth in taxes, 10% of the 100 eth will be distributed proportionally between Joe and Harry based on how many tokens they hold. If Joe & Harry are the only holders of 200,000+ $DUPE, Joe would receive 4.44 eth, Harry would receive 5.55 eth.

Revenue share from service taxes

Duplicate will collect taxes from coins created on the platform which will be added to a revenue share pool. Holders of 500,000 $DUPE will be eligible to claim 50% from the revenue share pool.Example: Joe creates 2 different coins with the 0.2 eth payment plan which includes 5% tax towards duplicate. Both coins generate 100 eth volume in total, this means duplicate made 5 eth from taxes, 50% of the 5 eth (2.5 eth) gets added to a pool. The total pool from all coins made on Duplicate that include taxes towards Duplicate, will be distributed to holders above 500,000 $DUPE via a claim.
